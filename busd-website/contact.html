About

Hello! I am Bus-D Designs. Currently I am a graphic design student in college. I am just starting to get my freelancing career going to help me gain experience and also to help pay for college. I am grateful that you made it to my website! I hope that you have enjoyed what you have seen so far and that you consider having me do some work for you!

As you can see from the pieces of work I have throughout my website, I have had the opportunity to make different designs in different styles of work. If you are in need of something that isn't shown, theres a chance I have still done work in it. As I continue to create different designs I will keep posting them here.

My main emphasis in my studies is User Interface/Experience. I am also studying coding to go along with this, so this entire website was created by me! I know for some that isn't too great of a feat, but as it's my first website that I've built from the ground up, I'm pretty proud of it!
