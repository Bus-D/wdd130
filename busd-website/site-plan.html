Overview

Purpose

To provide myself with a webite that I can begin to make a portfolio on. I plan to store my graphic design projects on it. Also I want to have a place that I can show my skills in website design and building.

Audience

My purpose is to provide people looking to hire me for work. They will be able to see both my graphic design work and also my website building skills. They will be able to contact me via the website and will be able to know what my prices are for doing work.

What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them?

The type and quality of work they can expect. On my site they will be able to see a portfolio of past work so they can get an idea of the quality and style of work that I can produce. They will be able to find a page that talks about my requirements and the process of hiring me to do a job for them. Along with that will be an area where they can get in contact with me to begin the process of communication for hiring me. There will also be a page for FAQâ€™s.

What types of activities will visitors want to complete on your site?

The activities visitors will see on my site will include an overview of my favorite works on the homepage, each of them will be linked to the page with all the portfolio that I will have uploaded. Each of the portfolio pieces will include with them a description of my process to make them, the goal the piece was aiming to accomplish, and how I feel the work fulfills the goal it was set out to accomplish.

FAQ

There will be a page that will be dedicated to providing information on how to get in contact with me. Along with this getting in contact will provide a rough idea of the contract they will be expected to sign when hiring me. This contract will include the pricing of work, and the expectation that the payment will come in three parts. 1) upon hiring 2) upon the sketches being provided 3) completed work. The contract will also state that revisions to any part of the design are allowed, but to a certain extent, upon reaching that extent or adding additional expectations to the workload there will be a discussion on the increase in pricing. If requested there will be a clause allowing for the client to back out at any time along the process, with the expectation that the client has no right to any designs or sketches up until that point. They will only have the right to publish or produce the final product after the final payment has been made.
